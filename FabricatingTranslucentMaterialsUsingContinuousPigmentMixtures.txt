FabricatingTranslucentMaterialsUsingContinuousPigmentMixtures

Authors: Gross, ETH Zurich

Purpose: to propose a method for practical physical reproduction and design
  of homogeneous materials with desired subsurface scattering of a given
  target material.

Subsurface Scattering : Light is emitted at a different angle than it enters
